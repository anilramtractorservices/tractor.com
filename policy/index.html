Our Policies

Terms of Service, Work Conditions, and General Information

1. General Service Terms

Anil Tractor Service offers local agricultural and utility tractor operations within Sheikhpura, Bihar, and adjacent fields. Bookings are scheduled based on field availability, route conditions, and machinery allocation. All field jobs must be booked in advance directly through our official phone, email, or physical visitation.

2. Weather & Ground Limitations

Tractor tasks, including farm cultivation, rotavator ploughing, and heavy transportation, rely heavily on favorable land and weather parameters. We reserve the full right to pause or reschedule ongoing commitments under heavy downpours, waterlogging, or severe ground conditions to prevent tractor damage or unsafe operations.

3. Pricing and Payment Terms

Service quotes depend entirely on field dimensions (per bigha/acre), specific implement attachments utilized, or distance covered for transport duties. Final dues must be settled as agreed upon completion of the requested job unless fixed parameters are discussed upfront.

4. Privacy & Personal Communication

When you drop your contact parameters, name, or phone data via email to book a service slot, we only utilize it to schedule, carry out, or follow up on your tractor requirements. Your information is never logged into secondary trackers, leased out, or sent to external marketers.
